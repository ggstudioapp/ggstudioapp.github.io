Privacy Policy

Personal Data:

  • Gg Studio respects your privacy rights and recognizes the importance of protecting any information collected about you.
  • Gg Studio do not collect any personal user data. Gg Studio may use tools or third-party analytics software to collect and use certain non-personal data that does not enable us to identify you (we track only data such as: device type, mobile device software, data carrier, ...).
  • Gg Studio uses Google Analytics to analyze anonymous aggregated usage data for our applications.

Non-Personal Data:

  • Gg Studio applications use Google AdMob ads as primary ad service and Facebook Audience Network, StartApp.
